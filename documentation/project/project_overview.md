# Bytes Smith — Website Project Overview

## Company

**Bytes Smith** is a Singapore-based AI company operating across three areas:

1. **AI Products** — Commercialized AI tools built for specific professional domains (e.g., legal, finance, healthcare, logistics).
2. **Custom AI Solutions** — Tailored AI tools and integrations built for clients to embed AI into their existing workflows.
3. **AI Services & Consultancy** — Model training, data labelling, and strategic AI consultancy for businesses exploring or scaling AI adoption.

---

## Website Goal

A simple, clean, small-business style static website that:
- Communicates what Bytes Smith does **within the first 30 seconds** of a visit
- Looks **professional but not complicated** — no clutter, no fluff
- Makes it easy for potential customers to understand the services and **take action** (contact, inquire)

---

## Target Audience

- SMEs and enterprises in Singapore (and regionally) looking to adopt AI
- Businesses with specific domain problems that could be solved with AI tools
- Companies seeking AI consulting or technical AI services (data, training, integration)

---

## Website Structure (Proposed)

### Pages
- **Home** — Hero with one-line value proposition, brief services overview, CTA
- **Services** — Three clear service pillars with short descriptions
- **Products** — Showcase of Bytes Smith's own AI tools (can start minimal)
- **About** — Company background, Singapore base, team (optional at launch)
- **Contact** — Simple contact form or email link

### Key Sections on Homepage
1. **Hero** — Company name, tagline, primary CTA ("Talk to us" / "See our services")
2. **Services snapshot** — Three icons/cards: AI Products | Custom AI | Services & Consultancy
3. **Why Bytes Smith** — 2–3 short trust points (e.g., domain-focused, Singapore-based, end-to-end)
4. **Contact / CTA strip** — Email or inquiry prompt

---

## Design Direction

Inspired by [purplerhino.co.uk](https://www.purplerhino.co.uk/) — a small business site that earns trust quickly through bold confident copy, a clean sectional layout, and a clear three-column service showcase. Adapted for an AI company context.

- **Style:** Clean, sectional layout — bold hero, three-column service cards, trust strip, CTA, footer
- **Color palette:** Deep navy `#0D1B2A` base + bright teal `#06B6D4` accent + white background + light grey `#F1F5F9` for alternating sections
- **Typography:** Inter (Google Fonts) — bold headings, readable body
- **Tone:** Confident, consultative, honest — "We give you exactly what you need" energy, no jargon
- **Copy approach:** Lead with the value to the customer, not features. Short sentences. Direct CTAs.
- **Responsive:** Mobile-first, works on all screen sizes
- **No frameworks** — plain HTML/CSS/JS only

---

## Tech Stack

- Static site (HTML + CSS + JS, or a lightweight generator like Astro/11ty/Hugo)
- No backend required at launch
- Hosted on a static hosting platform (e.g., Netlify, Vercel, GitHub Pages)

---

## Success Criteria

A visitor should be able to answer these three questions within 60 seconds of landing on the site:
1. What does Bytes Smith do?
2. Which service is relevant to me?
3. How do I get in touch?
