Our Story

About Bytes Smith

A Singapore-based team that believes AI should be practical, accessible, and genuinely useful for real businesses.

Who We Are

We make AI work for your business

Bytes Smith was founded with a simple belief: AI has the potential to transform how businesses operate, but most organisations don't have the time, resources, or expertise to figure it out on their own.

We're a team of AI engineers, domain specialists, and business problem-solvers based in Singapore. We focus on outcomes, not technology for its own sake — if AI isn't the right answer, we'll tell you that too.

Our work spans three areas: building AI products for specific professional sectors, developing custom AI solutions that integrate into clients' existing workflows, and providing AI services and consultancy for organisations at any stage of their AI journey.

How We Work

Our values in practice

These aren't words on a wall. They're the principles that guide every project we take on.

🎯

Problem First

We start with your business problem, not with a technology we want to apply. The right solution follows from a clear understanding of the challenge.

💬

Straight Talk

We give honest, direct advice. If something won't work, we say so. We'd rather lose a project than set a client up for failure.

🔧

Practical Execution

We build things that actually work in the real world — not polished prototypes that never make it to production.

🤝

Long-Term Partnership

We aim to be a trusted partner, not a one-off vendor. Our clients come back because the work delivers results.

Where We Are

Built in Singapore, for the region

Being based in Singapore gives us a grounded understanding of the regulatory environment, the pace of business in Southeast Asia, and the practical realities that regional companies face when adopting new technology.

We work with clients across Singapore and the wider APAC region, with the ability to support projects globally where the work suits our expertise.

Singapore's position as a technology and finance hub means we're close to the industries where AI can make the biggest near-term difference — and that's exactly where we focus our energy.
